FAQ

Questions, answered

Who can register on Campus Connect?
+
Any student or staff member at a participating institution can register. After submitting your details, an admin will review and approve your account before you can log in. This ensures only verified campus members gain access.
How does the admin approval process work?
+
When you register, your account is placed in a pending state. The campus admin reviews your submission and approves or rejects accordingly. Once approved, you receive access to your role-appropriate dashboard.
Can staff members create events and notices?
+
Yes. Staff accounts have elevated permissions to create events with capacity limits, publish notices, and manage lost & found entries. They can also view registration lists and payment statuses for events they manage.
How does the lost & found system work?
+
Students can report a lost item by describing it and where/when it was lost. Anyone who finds an item can post it as found. When a potential match exists, students submit a claim request which staff verify before releasing the item.
Is the payment module connected to events?
+
Yes. When an event has a fee, students must complete payment as part of registration. The payment module tracks status (pending, paid, failed) and admins can see a full financial record per student and per event.
What technology stack is Campus Connect built on?
+
Campus Connect is built using Django (Python) for the backend, MySQL as the database, and Bootstrap 5 for the front-end UI. Authentication uses Django's built-in user model extended with custom role fields and an approval workflow.
